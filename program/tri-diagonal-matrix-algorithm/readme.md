# Numerical-Calculation-Program - Tri-Diagonal-Matrix-Algorithm
## Algorithm

$$
\vec{A} =
\begin{pmatrix}
	a_{0, 0} & a_{0, 1} & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
	a_{1, 0} & a_{1, 1} & a_{1, 2} & \cdots & 0 & 0 & 0\\
	0 & a_{2, 1} & a_{2, 2} & \cdots & 0 & 0 & 0\\
	\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots
\end{pmatrix}
$$
